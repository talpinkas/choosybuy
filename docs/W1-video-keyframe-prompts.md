## 1. הצפה (Overwhelm)

```
A minimalist black-and-white 2D cartoon, simple clean hand-drawn line art, thick

(fresh youthful friendly face, casual sweater, relaxed hairstyle) from the
reference, at her desk with a laptop.

Action: she is overwhelmed as many clothing items (shirts, pants, dresses) pop out
of the laptop screen and multiply, floating and crowding all around her. A phone
rings nearby (small motion lines), and an older child arrives tugging at her
sleeve. She slumps, buried under the floating clutter, exhausted.

Color rule: the ENTIRE image is strictly black, white and gray, EXCEPT one element —
a small torn t-shirt in dull faded muted green resting on the desk. No other color.

Vertical 9:16. No text, no letters, no logos, no watermark, no UI.
```
